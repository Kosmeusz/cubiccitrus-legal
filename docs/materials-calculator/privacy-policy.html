Privacy Policy — Materials Calculator

Effective date: July 24, 2026
Contact: admin@cubiccitrus.com

Materials Calculator ("the App") is developed by Cubic Citrus. This policy explains what data the App collects and how it's used.

Summary

The App does not require an account, does not collect your name, email, or any other personal information, and stores all of your calculator data (segment inputs, material settings, saved job history) locally on your device only. That data is never transmitted anywhere and is deleted if you uninstall the App.

The two exceptions are the third-party services the App uses to show ads (free version) and process the optional Pro purchase, described below.

Data collected by third-party services

Advertising (Google AdMob) — free version only

If you're using the free version of the App, it displays ads served by Google AdMob. To do this, AdMob may collect:

  • Advertising identifier (IDFA on iOS / Google Advertising ID on Android)
  • Device information (device type, OS version)
  • IP address / approximate location
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)

This data is collected and processed by Google, not by us directly. You can review Google's practices in the Google Privacy Policy and manage ad personalization in your device's ad settings (Settings → Privacy → Ads on iOS, or Settings → Ads on Android).

Purchasing Pro removes all ads and stops this data collection.

In-App Purchase (Apple / Google)

The one-time "Pro" purchase is processed entirely by Apple's App Store or Google Play Billing. We never see or store your payment details — only a local flag on your device indicating you've unlocked Pro. Purchase and refund records are held by Apple/Google under their own privacy policies:

  • Apple Privacy Policy
  • Google Privacy Policy

Data we store ourselves

All of the following stays on your device (via local storage) and is never sent to us or anyone else:

  • Your concrete, tile, and paint calculator inputs (segments and material settings)
  • Your unit preferences (feet/meters, lbs/kg)
  • Your saved job history (Pro feature)
  • Whether you've unlocked Pro

Children's privacy

The App is a general-purpose utility not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.

Your choices

  • You can disable ad personalization in your device settings at any time.
  • You can delete all locally stored App data by uninstalling the App.
  • Purchasing Pro permanently removes ads and the associated data collection going forward.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this same URL with a revised effective date.
